Crafts
======
   
**Kids crafts**
---------------

What you will need:

Paint and markers

Cottonballs

Colorful beads and buttons that are different sizes

Colorful paper

Paper plates

Empty toilet paper rolls

Fuzzy bendy wire

Glue

Glitter

Wooden sticks

String

Scissors

All these supplys are cheap and can be found at a Walmart near you.
